Inworks Branding and Illustrations

Inworks is a Human-Centered Design school at the University of Colorado Denver. As their graphic illustrator, I was tasked with creating brand illustrations depicting their four tenets: design thinking, computational thinking, disruptive innovation, and radical interdisciplinarity. You can see them on the live Inworks site here .

UX Challenges:

I had to visualize four largely intangible concepts. Some of them have overlapping themes. There was also no precedent for illustrations in the Inworks branding, so I had free reign over styling.

UX Solutions:

My process started with definitions for each tenet from the Inworks white paper. I translated that into five key words that came either directly from the definition or from whatever popped into mind while reading it.
